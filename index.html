Experience Minecraft with enhanced features through these modded Eaglercraft versions. Each version offers unique modifications and improvements to the classic Minecraft experience.

From client-side performance enhancements to entirely new gameplay mechanics, these modded versions bring new life to the classic Minecraft experience in your browser.

Here are the steps on how to add mods to Eaglerforge and the Shadow Client. First, go to this website "https://eaglerrinth.github.io". Then click on the All APIs dropdown menu and select Old-Eaglerforge 1.8.8. Then choose the mod you want and select Download. Now open Eaglerforge and go to Mods. Finally select upload, upload your mod, and have fun.
